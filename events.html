HackXplore 2025

The Ultimate Inter-College Hackathon

HackXplore 2025 is a 24-hour inter-college hackathon designed to bring together innovative problem-solvers from diverse academic backgrounds to tackle real-world technological challenges. Participants will work in teams of 2 to 4 members to develop creative, impactful, and technically sound solutions.

Stages and Timelines

  • Round 1 (Online Submission): Oct 23, 2025 (8:00 AM) - Oct 28, 2025 (12:00 AM)
  • Round 2 (Offline Finale): Nov 01, 2025 (8:00 AM - 6:00 PM)
  • Registration Deadline: Oct 27, 2025 (12:00 AM)

Eligibility & Registration

  • Participation is open to students from all specializations and institutions across India.
  • Teams must consist of a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 4 members.
  • Inter-college and inter-specialization teams are allowed.

Rewards and Prizes (Prizes worth ₹50,000)

  • Winner: ₹25,000 + Certificate
  • First Runner Up: ₹15,000 + Certificate
  • Second Runner Up: ₹10,000 + Certificate
  • Participation Certificates for all finalists.

Judging Criteria

Submissions will be judged based on: Innovation & Creativity, Technical Implementation, Feasibility & Impact, Presentation & Clarity, and Business Development.
